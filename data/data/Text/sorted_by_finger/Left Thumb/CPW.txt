# Interpersonal And Innovation Potential (Left Thumb)

The ability to interact with others, including one’s interactive model, one’s sensitivity towards the emotion, motives and feelings of others, one’s ability to react differently to the emotions of others. Individuals with high interpersonal and innovation potential are more considerate and diplomatic. Besides that, this finger also represents one’s external execution ability such as the development and innovation of a new line of business or affair.

**Type**: CPW  
**%**: 0.00%  
**RC**: (0-Unlimited)

---

## test - Interpersonal And Innovation Potential

1. **Interactive model:** Good at taking the initiative and eager. Able to make many friends. Adapts easily.  
2. **Ability to develop new affairs:** Good at persuading, negotiating and convincing others. Able to relate one matter to the other.  
3. **Ability to deal with goals:** Goal-oriented. Determined to achieve goal once it is set. May have more than one goal at a time.  
4. **Active behavior in relationships:** Sensitive towards emotions, feelings and willingness of others. Enjoys coordinating and helping others. Adheres to situations easily. Good at handling relationships. Hardly causes stress to others.  
5. **External character:** Depends on one’s determination, goals and interests.

---

## Specific Suggestions To Improve test - Interpersonal And Innovation Potential

1. Train yourself to set goals and work towards them.  
2. Make your goals more concrete and focus on your goals to prevent wasting time and energy.  
3. If young, let child have more choices in order to learn how to make decisions, divide and manage time wisely.  
4. If young, let child make choices and decisions independently to instill a sense of responsibility.

---

## Other Suggestions

1. Participate in group activities or games (i.e. birthday parties, group visits during Lunar New Year, joining the Scout etc.).  
2. Participate in motivational camps, leadership camps etc.  
3. Develop interpersonal skills [learn how to make requests, express, listen, praise, encourage etc.].  
4. Vie to be committee member in organizations, take opportunities to make own self heard more often.  
5. Participate in religion, charity events, voluntary work (i.e. Red Cross Society, Go Green Campaign etc.).  
6. Spend 10–20 minutes daily to practice listening skills.  
7. Pay attention to the conversations among hosts and guests on the shows to improve on communication skills.  
8. Take the initiative to converse with others in public.  
9. Use Index Card or categorize name cards accordingly (relatives, friends, school mates, business acquaintances) and keep in touch with them.  
10. Learn in groups (study group, discussion group etc.).  
11. Attend seminars from time to time and make more friends.  
12. Organize a brainstorming group and have activities accordingly from time to time.  
13. Read more books on socialization, etiquette and etc.  
14. Participate in games or competitions that require team work.  
15. If young, parents should listen attentively to children after they finish school in order to understand more about their social situation in school.  
16. Learn how to make full use of the strengths of every team member and be more competitive.  
17. If young, instills moral values in child so that life can be more meaningful.
